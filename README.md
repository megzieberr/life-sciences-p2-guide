# Life Sciences Paper 2 — Interactive Study Guide

A colourful, gamified single-page study guide for **Grade 12 Life Sciences Paper 2**
(Curro Kathu · 2026), compiled from 10+ years of IEB past papers and marking guidelines.

## 📖 Live site

Once GitHub Pages finishes building, the guide is available at:

**https://megzieberr.github.io/life-sciences-p2-guide/**

## ✨ What's inside

Eight sections covering the full demarcation:

1. **Overview** — paper format, mark allocation, exam-day strategy
2. **Flowering Plants** — structure, pollination, fertilisation, seeds & fruit, germination, auxins, seed banks
3. **Endocrine System** — all glands & hormones, homeostasis, negative feedback, blood glucose, thyroid, sperm production
4. **Human Reproduction** — male & female systems, sperm structure, spermatogenesis, oogenesis, fertilisation, pregnancy, contraception
5. **Menstrual Cycle** — phase-by-phase, all four hormones, PCOS, STIs
6. **Disorders** — thyroid, pituitary, pancreas (diabetes) and adrenal disorders
7. **Practice Q&A** — real IEB questions with model answers (click to reveal)
8. **Tricks & Traps** — the 20 most common student errors, repeated column-match pairs, True/False traps

## 🎮 Study features

- **Progress tracking** — mark each section as studied; your progress saves automatically in the browser
- **Confetti reward** when you complete all 8 sections 🎉
- Smooth scroll-reveal animations, colour-coded topics, and a back-to-top button
- Works on phone, tablet and desktop — open the link and study anywhere

## 🛠️ Tech

A single self-contained `index.html` file — no build step, no dependencies. Just open it in any browser.
